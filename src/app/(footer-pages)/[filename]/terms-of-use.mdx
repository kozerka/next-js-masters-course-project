# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains the rules regarding the processing and protection of personal data provided by users in connection with the use of our services.

## Data processing principles

User personal data is processed in accordance with applicable laws, in particular with the Regulation of the European Parliament and of the Council (EU) on the protection of natural persons with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data (GDPR). Personal data is stored on secure servers, with measures ensuring a high level of privacy protection.

## User rights

Every user has the right to access their data, the possibility of rectifying them, deleting or limiting processing, as well as the right to object, the right to data portability, and the right to withdraw consent to the processing of personal data at any time without affecting the legality of the processing carried out on the basis of consent before its withdrawal.

## Data retention period

Users' personal data are stored for the period necessary to achieve specific purposes for which they were collected, in particular in order to perform the contract, until the consent is withdrawn or an objection to the processing of personal data is raised.
