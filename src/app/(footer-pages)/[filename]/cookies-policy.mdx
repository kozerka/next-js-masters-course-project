# Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies to track visits on our website. By browsing our site, you agree to the use of cookies in accordance with this Policy.

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that can be used by websites to make a user's experience more efficient. The law allows us to store cookies on your device if they are strictly necessary for the operation of this site. For all other types of cookies, we need your consent.

## How do we use cookies?

We use cookies to understand how you use our site and to improve your browsing experience. Cookies can store your preferences and other information but are not used to identify you.

## Your choices regarding cookies

You can decide whether to allow cookies to be stored on your device. You can change your cookie settings at any time by adjusting your browser settings to reject some or all cookies. However, blocking all cookies may affect the availability of some features of our site.

## Changes to our Cookie Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. We encourage you to regularly review this page to stay informed of any changes.
